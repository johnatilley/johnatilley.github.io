title: I'm starting a Youtube Garden journal

My partner and I recently moved house and it comes with an amazing long garden. I've always been into gardening and now is the perfect opportunity to take it to the next level.

The places I've lived in before tend to have been flats so access to a garden has been a challenge. In my last house I managed to get some green fingers but I spent so much of the time repairing a weedy lawn that I got burned out really quickly, plus that garden was really small.

So now I have a long urban garden and it has so much potential. The previous owners clearly did a lot of work on it and I have plans to carry on maintaining the flower beds whilst changing the layout and hardscaping to suit us.

This is where the new youtube series comes in. A video journal, or Vlog if you prefer, where I document the Garden and the changes it will go through over the next year as I reshape the lawn and rebuild the structures. It's a very exciting time!

My presentation and video editing clearly need some work, but I'm a big believer in learning and improving whilst gaining the experience by taking action. There's only so much you can get from theory that you just have to get in front of a camera and start blabbing away, and the more you do it the more you become natural and comfortable with it, because let's face it, talking to a black rectangle is kind of weird.
